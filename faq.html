Frequently Asked Questions

All the clarity, science, and trust-building answers you need — so you can list smarter with confidence.

▶ Psychology & AI

AutoList Canada uses behavioral science to guide every listing suggestion. Titles, descriptions, tags, and prices are generated using trust signals, clarity principles, and emotional resonance logic — making your items more clickable and conversion-ready.

▶ Privacy & Data

Your listings and login data are securely stored and never shared. We comply with Canadian data protection guidelines and prioritize user control.

▶ Pricing & Plans

We offer a free tier for beginners, plus paid plans for pros and agencies. Monthly options include AI credits, crosslist syncs, and more. Visit our Pricing page for details.

▶ Crosslisting Logic

AutoList Canada supports eBay, Poshmark, Etsy, Facebook Marketplace, Depop, Shopify, and more. Our sync logic ensures each listing matches the platform's style and buyer psychology. You remain in control of edits and delisting.

▶ Support & Features

Need help with listings, syncing, or your account? Our team is here. Click the button below or email us directly at support@autolistcanada.ca.

▶ Do I need to use AI?

Nope — all AI tools are optional. But we believe they give sellers a major edge by removing guesswork and boosting listing quality.

▶ Will it work with French listings?

Yes. AutoList Canada is designed for bilingual sellers. Our AI tools can analyze and optimize French-language listings with clear structure and tone logic.

▶ How do I cancel or switch plans?

You can manage your subscription anytime via the dashboard. Plans are flexible, and there’s no penalty for canceling or upgrading.
