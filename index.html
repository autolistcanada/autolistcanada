Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Q Do I need to connect marketplace accounts to use AutoList Canada?

No. All tools are available without connecting your platforms. AutoList Canada is designed to respect your workflow and data privacy — no integrations required unless you choose them.

Q Is this only for large shops or full-time sellers?

Not at all. AutoList Canada supports solo resellers, part-time sellers, and multi-channel teams. Whether you're scaling or just getting started, the tools adapt to your needs.

Q How is AutoList Canada different from other crosslisting software?

We combine behavioral science, market-specific logic, and clarity-first design to support intentional growth. This isn't just about crossposting — it's about building smarter workflows and sustainable momentum.

Q Will AutoList Canada change or post my listings automatically?

Never without permission. You're in full control of when, how, and where your content is synced. Every action is optional, transparent, and fully editable.
