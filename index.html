Psychology & AI

Our AI is designed using cognitive science, tone psychology, and clarity compression — helping your listings resonate faster with the right buyer. Unlike black-box AI, we disclose exactly how each tool works and optimize for Canadian markets.

Privacy & Data

We do not sell, rent, or scrape your data — ever. Listings are yours alone. AutoList is built with privacy-first architecture and zero ad tracking.

Pricing & Plans

Plans start at $0 for casual sellers, with Pro and Agency tiers offering full automation, analytics, and listing syncing. See our pricing page for full details.

Crosslisting Logic

Listings are pushed across platforms via API or Chrome extension (depending on your plan). We support eBay, Poshmark, Etsy, Facebook Marketplace (manual), and more coming soon.

Support & Features

You can access support via the Contact Support button below. Feature suggestions are welcome and regularly reviewed for roadmap inclusion.

AI Credit System

Each subscription includes monthly AI credits (e.g., 50 for Starter, 200 for Pro). These are used when generating titles, descriptions, tags, and other AI-enhanced fields. You’ll always see your balance on the dashboard.

Security & Trust Protections

We implement SSL encryption, form validation, and database protection. Listings cannot be accessed by third parties. Trust scoring models are based on psychological safety, not manipulation.

Account & Billing

You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time. We use Gumroad and LemonSqueezy for secure payment processing. Invoices and billing settings can be managed through your account dashboard.
