Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 10, 2025

AutoList Canada is committed to protecting your privacy and building trust with every visitor. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user of our platform.

1. Introduction

AutoList Canada (“we”, “us”, “our”) operates the autolist.ca website and related services. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our site.

2. What We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone (if provided by you)
  • Device & Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device info, site usage analytics
  • Listings Data: Information you submit about vehicles, including photos and descriptions
  • AI Interactions: Content you submit to our AI tools or chat features

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To personalize your experience
  • To communicate with you (if you opt in)
  • To monitor, secure, and analyze site usage
  • To develop new features, including AI-powered tools

4. AI & Automation

AutoList Canada uses AI to enhance listings, provide recommendations, and power our chat and search features. AI tools only use the information you provide and do not access private data without your consent.

5. Data Protection

  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • We use secure cloud infrastructure and regular security audits
  • Access to your data is strictly controlled

6. Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Opt out of marketing or analytics
  • Contact us for privacy-related requests at privacy@autolist.ca

7. Cookies

We use cookies to remember your preferences and analyze site traffic. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

8. AI Opt-Out

If you wish to opt out of AI-powered features or data processing, please contact us at privacy@autolist.ca.

9. Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies before providing any information.

10. Contact Us

For questions or concerns about your privacy, contact our team at privacy@autolist.ca.
